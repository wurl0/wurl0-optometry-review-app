1. The cardinal points, and the nodal ray

Light enters from the left. The six cardinal points come in three pairs: focal (F, F′), principal (P, P′), and nodal (N, N′). Tap a point for its role. Drag the visual angle to send a distant object through the nodal point and watch the retinal image form.

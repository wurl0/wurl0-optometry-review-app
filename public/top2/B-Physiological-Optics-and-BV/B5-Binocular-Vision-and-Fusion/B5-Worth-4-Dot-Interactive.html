Worth 4-Dot Test — Interactive

Board Review 2026 · Personal study notes · red filter over the RIGHT eye, green over the left

Four lights: one red on top, two green at the sides, one white at the bottom. The red filter lets the right eye see only red (white looks red), the green filter lets the left eye see only green. What the patient reports tells you about fusion, suppression, or diplopia.

Summary table

4 lightsNormal fusion (or harmonious ARC at near)
2 red lightsLeft eye (green) suppressed
3 green lightsRight eye (red) suppressed
5 lightsDiplopia (no fusion): red on the right = eso/uncrossed, red on the left = exo/crossed

Test distance matters: at 33 cm it checks a large area (central + peripheral fusion); at 6 m only the small central area, so it is more likely to reveal a small central suppression scotoma.
